BUSH TETRAS REALEASE NEW SINGLE “THEY LIVE IN MY HEAD”.

Over eleven tracks, the record blends wiry guitars, hypnotic rhythms, and Cynthia Sley’s razor-edged vocals into something both raw and elegiac. The songs feel like fragments of dreams and flashbacks, evoking the ghosts of New York, lost friends, and a world in flux.

Born between Brooklyn, New York and Historic New Castle, Delaware, Wharf Cat Records has been quietly shaping the sound of the American underground since 2011. The label’s identity is rooted in an art-punk aesthetic — raw, refined, and defiantly off-center. Each release feels like a complete work of art, where sound and visuals merge into one vision.

At its core, Wharf Cat is a community of artists who push beyond genre, balancing chaos and precision, instinct and intention. Whether it’s post-punk, experimental rock, or something that doesn’t fit anywhere at all, the label celebrates honesty and artistic risk over trends and algorithms.
